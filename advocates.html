You Just Found an OpenAAC Supporter!

The person or group who linked to this page is helping spread the word about the importance of Openness for AAC users, families and practitioners!

AAC (short for Agumentative and Alternative Communication) are tools, apps and strategies that can help people with disabilities or inconsistent speech to still get their message across. Speech-generating apps, or printed boards or cards are good examples of AAC.

OpenAAC is about advocating for more access, flexibility and support for AAC users. Too many AAC solutions right now are proprietary, limited to a specific solution, or not respectful of the communicator's right to choose. OpenAAC is about gathering the resources and solutions to change all that, and the project, group or person who sent you here is helping to get the word out! You can too!
